Local Food

Sego Gegog

Sego Gegok is a traditional rice dish from Trenggalek served in a unique way. It is made from rice wrapped in banana leaves and steamed until fully cooked. This dish is complemented with anchovy sambal, which gives it a distinctive spicy and savory flavor. The preparation process begins by cooking the rice until half done, then wrapping it in banana leaves together with the anchovy sambal before steaming it again until perfectly cooked.

Alen-alen

Alen-alen is a light snack shaped like a ring with an enticing distinctive yellow color. Made from cassava starch mixed with spices such as turmeric, coriander, and garlic, this snack has a crispy texture with a unique savory flavor. The preparation process involves carefully mixing the ingredients to achieve the right dough texture before shaping and frying it until golden brown.

Manco

Manco comes in two varieties: sesame manco and glutinous rice manco. This traditional cake is made from glutinous rice flour carefully prepared to produce a distinctive texture. The preparation process involves mixing glutinous rice flour with sugar and other ingredients, then shaping it into various forms such as small blocks, triangles, or rounds before sprinkling with sesame seeds.

Nasi Tiwul

Nasi Tiwul is a traditional dish made from processed cassava that is dried and ground into a fine powder. The preparation process is quite lengthy, starting with drying the cassava, grinding it into flour, and then steaming it until it becomes Nasi Tiwul, ready to be served.
